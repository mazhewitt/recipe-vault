Chilli (Vegetarian)
ingredients:
3-4 cloves of garlic
1 Onion
1 red habanero
6 x 3x5cm3 in chilli in adobo
4-5 frozen (aprox)
Rapeseed oil (Raps ol)
1 thing of Quorn
1 tin/can of kidney beans
1 tin/can of pulped/chopped Tomato
tiny bit of water to wash the rest of the
tomato into pan.
--> to have with rice
Difficulty
1/5
Serves
Prep time
Cooking time
Wine pairings
181
preparation
1. Chop garlic and chilli (habaneros) finely.
2. Chop chilli in adobo (if not frozen, then use 1-2 chilli(s) from the can and a few teaspoons of the adobo sauce, instead).
3. Dice Onion.
4. Rinse kidney beans.
5. Put a little more than you would think of the Rapeseed oil in a pan (one of these, not a frying pan or pot) and turn heat to 7/8.
6. Put diced onions in to the pan, cook until the onions are clear (approx 5 min.) (don't burn them).
7. At this point, add chopped garlic, chilli and chilli in adobo into the pan with the onion.
8. Cook until garlic is cooked for a bit (not burned) --> a few minutes, max 5 min).
9. Add thing of Quorn into mix and turn heat to 6.
10. Cook till properly mixed in (especially when frozen).
11. Then add rinsed kidney beans.
12. Add tinned tomato (not whole, chopped/pulped).
13. Taste (check if everything is alright).
14. Add a lid, turn heat to 3 or 4.
15. Let cook for aprox 40 min.
Note: heat settings for an induction hob.
